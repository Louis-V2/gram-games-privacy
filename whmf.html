WHMF — Who Has More Followers?

Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

WHMF ("the App") is a free mobile trivia game developed by Gram Games. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require account creation, login, or any personally identifiable information to use.

The only data stored is your game progress (best streak), which is saved locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Serves advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Processes in-app purchases (Remove Ads). RevenueCat may collect anonymous transaction data. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — Handles app distribution and purchases according to their respective privacy policies.

Advertising

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. You may see personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your device settings and consent choices. You can purchase "Remove Ads" to eliminate all advertising from the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain. Game progress stored locally on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
